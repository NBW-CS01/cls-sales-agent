Customer: NickNack

Company Background:
NickNack is a leading innovator in the world of bespoke tiles, crafting beautiful and durable surfaces for both commercial and residential spaces. From hand-finished mosaics to large-format architectural tiles, NickNack supplies premium designs to architects, builders, and interior designers across the UK and beyond. Behind the artistry, however, lies a data challenge of monumental scale. Over years of rapid growth, NickNack has amassed around one petabyte of design, production, and logistics data stored in Amazon S3.
